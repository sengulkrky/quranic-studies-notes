#### Righteousness according to 2:177
- Believe in Allah, the last day, the angels, the books, the prophets
- Give charity to relatives, orphans, the poor, needy travellers, beggars and for freeing captives
- Establish prayer, pay alms-tax, keep the pledges you make
- Be patient in times of suffering, adversity and in the heat of battle
